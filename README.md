## 🚀 Power BI Project: Airline Performance Dashboard
Built an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze airline operational and financial performance using flight-level data.

**📊 Dataset**
1. The dataset belongs to the Aviation / Airline Industry.

2. It contains flight-level information such as routes, passengers, revenue, delays, and profit.

3. The goal was to analyze airline performance across routes and regions to identify operational and financial trends.

**📈 Dashboard Built**

I developed an Airline Performance Dashboard in Power BI focusing on:

1. Flight revenue and profit analysis

2. Passenger traffic trends

3. Route and regional performance

4. Delay analysis and operational efficiency

**🔎 Key Business Insights**

1. International routes generate higher revenue and profit compared to domestic routes on average.

2. Flights with higher passenger loads tend to produce higher revenue, highlighting the importance of seat utilization.

3. Flight delays vary significantly, indicating operational inefficiencies in certain routes.

4. Identifying high-profit routes vs. low-profit routes helps airlines optimize route planning.

**🎯 Who Can Use This Dashboard?**

This dashboard can be useful for:

1. Airline management teams to monitor profitability and operational performance.

2. Operations teams to track delays and improve scheduling efficiency.

3. Business analysts to identify revenue opportunities and route optimization strategies.

💡 Tools Used: Power BI, Data Modeling, DAX, Data Visualization
